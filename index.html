    • Project Overview

    Our project explores how a machine learning model can detect whether someone is smiling or not using real-time webcam data. Built with Google's Teachable Machine, the model distinguishes between "Smile" and "Neutral" facial expressions. Inspired by Unmasking AI by Joy Buolamwini, our goal is not only technical but also ethical — examining how bias, lighting, and facial diversity affect how well AI systems "see" us. This site contains the model, our reflections, and thoughts on AI fairness.

    Our Approach

    We trained our model using Google's Teachable Machine platform, carefully collecting diverse examples of smiling and neutral faces. Our process included:

    • Collecting image samples across different lighting conditions
    • Testing with users of different backgrounds
    • Evaluating for potential biases in recognition accuracy
    • Refining the model through multiple iterations
